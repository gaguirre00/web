Our Story

Hi, I'm Ceci —
your soap maker

What started as a hobby in my kitchen quickly became a passion. After struggling to find soaps gentle enough for sensitive skin, I decided to make my own — and I never looked back.

Every bar begins with a carefully chosen blend of organic oils — coconut, olive, shea — paired with botanical additives like oatmeal, raw honey, or dried flowers. I mix, pour, and cure each batch myself, ensuring every piece meets my standards before it reaches your door.

When you buy a bar of Ceci's Jabón, you're not just buying soap. You're supporting a small dream — and treating your skin to something truly special.

  • No artificial fragrances or dyes
  • pH-balanced for all skin types
  • Made in small batches for freshness
  • Thoughtful, minimal packaging
